Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Jarilo LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our apps.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information on our own servers or systems.

However, certain data may be collected automatically by third-party platforms and services we use:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — When you download or purchase within our apps, Apple or Google collect information according to their own privacy policies. We receive only aggregate, anonymized sales and performance data from these platforms.
  • Expo — Our apps are built with Expo, which may collect limited diagnostic and usage data to support app functionality. See Expo's privacy policy at expo.dev/privacy.
  • Advertising — Our apps display ads to support free access. Ad networks may collect device identifiers (such as your advertising ID) and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).

2. In-App Purchases

Our apps offer optional in-app purchases (such as removing ads or unlocking additional content). These transactions are processed entirely by Apple or Google — we do not collect or store your payment information. Any purchase status or game progress is saved locally on your device or, where applicable, through your Google Play Games or Apple Game Center account.

3. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Our apps do not target children. If you believe a child has been exposed to advertising that collected their data, please contact the relevant ad network directly or use your device's parental controls.

4. Data We Never Collect

To be explicit, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Location data
  • Health or financial data
  • User-generated content
  • Any data on our own backend systems

5. Third-Party Links and Services

Our apps may interact with third-party services (Apple, Google, Expo, ad networks). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services and encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

6. Your Choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads on iOS via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or on Android via Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Reset your advertising ID at any time through your device settings.
  • Purchase the ad-free version of our app to remove ads entirely.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
